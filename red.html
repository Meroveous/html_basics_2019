MY Top 5 Restaurants!

Here are the top 5 places to eat ion the D/FW Area! Eat at your own risk!

1. Fogo De Chao

MEATY!

This restaurant appeals to the more carnivorous among us. All you can eat meat!!

2. The Mansion

PRICY!

Appeals to those who don't need to worry about the size of the bill. Better bring your Gold Card!

3. Olive Garden

COZY!

Nice family dining. You're gonna love the all you can eat 'soup and salad'!

4. Cracker Barrel

HOMESTYLE!

Don't try to find a seat at noon on Sunday. Might end up spending more on gifts than on food.

5. McDonalds

QUICKIE!

When you're running late, it's the place to go when ya need a quickie!!
